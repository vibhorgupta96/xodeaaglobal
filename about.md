Xodeaa Global Private Limited – International Construction & Infrastructure Workforce Solutions

Company Profile

Xodeaa Global Private Limited is a specialized international manpower recruitment and workforce solutions company focused on serving the Construction, Infrastructure, Civil Engineering, Industrial Projects, and Building Development sectors worldwide. Based in New Delhi, India, the company is dedicated to supplying highly skilled, semi-skilled, and general construction workers to contractors, developers, engineering firms, infrastructure companies, and project owners across Europe, the Middle East, and other international markets.

As global construction and infrastructure development continue to accelerate, companies around the world face increasing challenges in sourcing qualified, reliable, and project-ready workers. Xodeaa Global addresses this challenge by providing comprehensive manpower solutions tailored specifically to the construction industry. The company recruits, screens, mobilizes, and deploys workforce professionals who possess the technical expertise, practical experience, and work ethic required to support major construction and infrastructure projects.

Our goal is not simply to fill vacancies but to become a long-term workforce partner for international construction companies seeking dependable manpower solutions that contribute to project success, productivity, and operational excellence.
