Company Profile

The Workforce Behind Global Development

Xodeaa Global Private Limited is a specialized international manpower recruitment and workforce solutions company focused on the Construction, Infrastructure, Civil Engineering, Industrial Projects and Building Development sectors worldwide. Based in New Delhi, India, we supply highly skilled, semi-skilled and general construction workers to contractors, developers, engineering firms and project owners across Europe, the Middle East and beyond.

As global construction accelerates, companies face increasing challenges sourcing qualified, reliable and project-ready workers. We address this with comprehensive manpower solutions tailored to the construction industry — recruiting, screening, mobilizing and deploying professionals with the technical expertise and work ethic that major projects demand.

“Our goal is not simply to fill vacancies, but to become a long-term workforce partner that contributes to project success, productivity and operational excellence.”
